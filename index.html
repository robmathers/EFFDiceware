EFF Diceware Passphrase Generator

Generate a Readable, Secure Passphrase

We should all be using secure passwords, and password managers are great at it. But sometimes you need a memorable and readable passphrase (say, for your Wi-Fi). Diceware has long been a good way to do so securely. The EFF has a Diceware word list designed for usability. This tool uses that list, and true random numbers from Random.org, to produce a readable, secure and memorable passphrase.
